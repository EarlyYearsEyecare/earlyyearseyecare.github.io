Consultation times will be at least 90 minutes with each child or young person. The techniques to fully assess the true optical state of the eyes can be discussed in advance.

Friendly

In advance of any appointment, our optometrist is happy to show children and their parent(s)/guardian(s) around the practice in order to familiarise themselves with the environment, which although clinical is as friendly and relaxed as possible!

Pressure-free

Early Years Eyecare specialise in clinical optometry. You will not be put under any pressure to have spectacles or any other optical appliance dispensed with us.

What is myopia?

Progressive myopia, also known as short-sightedness, has rapidly become a major visual problem globally. It is especially associated with prolonged near vision work for example using laptops, tablets and mobile phones.

How can we help?

Early Years Eyecare assesses and practices “myopia control”. Personalised advice will be given and some individuals may benefit from specialised myopia control lenses.
